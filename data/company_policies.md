# Company Policies and Guidelines

## Overview

This document outlines the core policies and guidelines for all company employees.

## Working Hours

Regular working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. All employees are expected to be present during these core hours unless prior arrangements have been made with their manager.

### Flexible Work Arrangements

The company supports flexible working arrangements where feasible. Employees interested in flexible schedules should discuss this with their direct manager.

### Remote Work Policy

Remote work is available for eligible positions. Employees working remotely must maintain regular communication with their team and be available during core working hours.

## Leave and Time Off

Employees are entitled to 20 days of annual paid leave. Additional leave categories include sick leave, personal leave, and parental leave.

### Annual Leave

Annual leave should be planned in advance and approved by the employee's manager. During peak business periods, leave requests may be restricted.

### Sick Leave

Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. Notification should be given as soon as possible when taking unplanned sick leave.

## Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to maintain professional conduct at all times. This includes:

- Treating colleagues with respect and dignity
- Avoiding discrimination and harassment
- Maintaining confidentiality of company information
- Following all applicable laws and regulations

## Health and Safety

The company is committed to maintaining a safe workplace for all employees. All health and safety policies must be followed strictly.

### Workplace Safety

Employees must report any unsafe conditions or incidents immediately to their manager or the health and safety officer.

### Mental Health Support

The company provides mental health support resources and encourages employees to seek help if needed.

## Professional Development

The company invests in employee development through training programs, mentoring, and educational support.

### Training Programs

Employees are encouraged to participate in relevant training programs. The company provides an annual training budget for professional development.

## Discipline and Grievance

The company maintains fair and transparent processes for addressing discipline and grievance matters.
